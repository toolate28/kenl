# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We pledge to make participation in this project and our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of background or identity.

## Standards
Examples of behavior that contribute to a positive environment include: showing empathy and kindness to others, being respectful of differing opinions, and gracefully accepting constructive criticism.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, discrimination, doxxing, and sustained disruption of discussions.

## Enforcement
Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing the standards. For code of conduct violations, contact the maintainers listed in maintainers.md or use the security contact in SECURITY.md.
